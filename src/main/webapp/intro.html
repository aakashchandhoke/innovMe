INTRODUCTION TO INNOVME!

InnovME is a Manufacturing & Vendor Management, Quality Control and Product Development Company based in mainland China & India

From concept idea to market, InnovME is a one-stop shop incorporating design, development, prototyping, tooling, production, quality control, certification and logistics.With access to a wide network of resources comprising of the best China & India manufacturers and suppliers, the team from InnovME guarantees professionalism and credibility in every endeavor.

We are able to offer a highly coveted amalgam of a stringent dedication to deliver quality from the West and the advantage of Chinese & Indian prices. InnovME also offers a host of product/service sourcing solutions through a complex channel of suppliers, manufacturing companies in China & India and quality control experts to ensure clients are able to design, develop, produce and sell their branded goods without any hassled faced.

• Product design and development services • Prototyping solutions including the latest 3D printing techniques.

• Quality control, verification, factory audit, certification and inspection systems
