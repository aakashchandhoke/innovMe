Your Office

If you are looking to enter the Chinese & Indian market but are worried about the risks involved due to, amongst other things, distance from base, lack of local knowledge, and registered capital required to open a company then InnovME can help give you your initial presence in India & China through our Managed Outsourcing services.

Through our companies in Delhi & Hong Kong we are able to offer a full range of services to allow you to place a foot into India & China without having to dive fully in.

For a one off setup cost and monthly charge InnovME can give you the following:

1. A China &India presence with legal office & delivery address & contact number(if needed)
2. Legal employees working Full Time on your business
3. Computer infrastructure and server space in China & India
4. Desk space and fully equipped meeting facilities
5. Administration behind your employees for all HR and local finance related matters.

Also for additional one-off fees we can aid in management of certain projects and negotiations & management of contacts and letters of intent.

This is by far the cheapest and easiest way for you to get a taster of the Indian & Chinese markets and give you the information you need to make the big decision on whether to enter India & China or not.

Each proposal is unique to your situation, whether you need 1 employee or 10. We will also sign to say that you can keep the employees if you decide to enter India & China yourselves at a later date.

Contact us for a proposal giving details of your current business and what you are looking to achieve in India & China and we can then start discussing in detail how InnovME can help.
