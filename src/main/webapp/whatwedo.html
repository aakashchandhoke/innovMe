What we do?

We offer a scope of China and India based administrations from assembling, manufacturing and vendor administration, Quality control and reassurance, engineering, designing and prototyping.

We are adaptable in our approach and commonly work inside one of the below accompanying structures concerning our administrations:

Option 1: All-In Supply Solutions:

InnovME overlays the above All-in Supply Solution service package over an existing supplier for an agreed margin % per product. Payments for goods can continue factory direct or be processed through InnovME. Direct factory communications may continue if required and the service package can be stripped back to only cover specific project management elements/tasks or quality checks. Typical service fees range between 5-15% depending on the service requirements, product complexity, factory location, length of contract and order quantity.

Option 2: Overlay Service Plan:

InnovME overlays the above All-in Supply Solution service package over an existing supplier for an agreed margin % per product. Payments for goods can continue factory direct or be processed through InnovME. Direct factory communications may continue if required and the service package can be stripped back to only cover specific project management elements/tasks or quality checks. Typical service fees range between 5-15% depending on the service requirements, product complexity, factory location, length of contract and order quantity.

Option 3: Standalone Man-Day Services:

This can include PM related services, facility audits, quality control inspections or quality assurance activities. InnovME will undertake specific tasks on a day-by- day basis billing for time and related expenses as they are incurred (all man-days will be approved by you or your team before being undertaken).

The InnovME man-day rate for a standard QC engineer or PM is $205 per day (8-10 hours) all-inclusive for activities within the following regions:-
China,Zhuhai HQ, Guangdong Province, Zhongshan, Guangdong Province, Shenzhen, Guangdong Province, Guangzhou, Guangdong Province, Foshan, Guangdong Province, Dongguan, Guangdong Province, Ningbo, Zhejiang Province, Ninghai, Zhejiang Province, Jinhua, Zhejiang Province, Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, Zhangjiagang, Jiangsu Province, Suzhou, Jiangsu Province, Nantong, Jiangsu Province, Shanghai, Wendeng, Shandong Province, Weihai, Shandong Province, Delhi – NCR, UP, Haryana, Punjab, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Gujrat.
Factory locations outside of these regions will be subject to direct expense charges billed at cost.

Option 4: Standalone Man-Day Services – Rolling Contract:

Services rendered would be as per those outlined in Option 3 but you would commit to a long-term man-day requirement being either a set minimum number of days per month over a 6-12 month period or a block commitment to a set number of man-days. This would then enable InnovME to apply a discount to the standard day-rate charge.

Option 5: Fulltime Operatives in China:

This package would involve the specific recruitment and hire of fulltime operative(s) (PM or QC) that would work exclusively for you in China, legally employed, trained and managed through InnovME. Rates for such a service would be dependent on the personnel profile, level of InnovME oversight required and would require a long-term commitment to an employment contract per operative (minimum 12 months).
